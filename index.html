FAQ

Know Before You Start

(1) What is your design process like? ↓

My design process is user-centered and iterative. I start with research to understand user needs and business goals, followed by wireframing and prototyping. After that, I move into visual design and usability testing, refining the product based on feedback. The goal is not just to make things look good, but to ensure they work effectively for real users.

(2) How long does it take to complete a design project? ↓

Timelines vary depending on the complexity of the project. A simple landing page may take 1–2 weeks, while a full product design (like a web or mobile app) can take 4–8 weeks or more. Clear requirements and timely feedback significantly speed up the process.

(3) What services do you offer? ↓

I offer a wide range of design services, including UI/UX design, mobile app design, responsive website design, and front-end development. My focus goes beyond visuals—I create user-centered, high-performing digital experiences that improve usability, increase engagement, and help transform your business for long-term growth.

(4) How do you ensure good user experience in your designs? ↓

I use user research, testing, and real data to guide my designs. Instead of guessing, I check how users interact and improve the design based on their feedback to make it easy and effective.
